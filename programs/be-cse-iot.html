Program Overview

The B.E. in CSE (IoT, Cyber Security including Blockchain) at SNS College of Technology is a future-focused program combining three critical pillars of modern technology. Using **Design Thinking**, we train students to build secured, connected ecosystems and distributed trust systems, preparing them for the next generation of the decentralized internet.

Key Specializations & Subjects

Internet of Things (IoT) Protocols
Cyber Security & Threat Defense
Blockchain Technology & Smart Contracts
Ethical Hacking & Cryptography
Distributed Ledger Systems

Design Thinking in CSE (IoT/CS/BC)

Connectivity and security must be designed with the user in mind. At SNS, our Design Thinking framework guides students to build IoT devices and security protocols that are not just technically sound, but also intuitive and trustworthy. We teach them to empathize with the privacy concerns of users while innovating in the blockchain and cyber security space.
