Food Technology

Program Overview

The B.Tech. in Food Technology at Dr. SNS Rajalakshmi College of Arts and Science is dedicated to the science of nutrition and food production. Guided by **Design Thinking**, we empower students to innovate in food processing, preservation, and quality control, ensuring they are equipped to meet the global demand for safe, nutritious, and sustainable food solutions.

Key Specializations & Subjects

Food Preservation & Processing
Food Microbiology & Chemistry
Quality Control & Safety (HACCP)
Food Packaging Technology
Sustainable Food Production

Design Thinking in B.Tech. FT

Food technology is about solving the fundamental human need for nutrition and taste. At SNS, our Design Thinking framework encourages students to empathize with consumers and environmental needs. We teach them to prototype food products and processes that are not only efficient but also culturally resonant and environmentally sustainable.
