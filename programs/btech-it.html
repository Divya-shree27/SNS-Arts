Program Overview

The B.Tech. in Information Technology at Dr. SNS Rajalakshmi College of Arts and Science focuses on the management and processing of information in the digital age. Powered by **Design Thinking**, our students gain expertise in enterprise solutions, web technologies, and mobile applications, becoming the silent architects of the modern digital world.

Key Specializations & Subjects

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
Mobile App Development
Cyber Laws & Information Security
Web Services & Architecture
Data Warehousing & Management

Design Thinking in B.Tech. IT

Information technology is about making information accessible and useful. At SNS, we use Design Thinking to teach students how to organize and present data in ways that are intuitive for the user. We focus on empathizing with organizational needs to design IT systems that are not only robust but also user-centric.
