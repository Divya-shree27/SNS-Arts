The Master of Business Administration (MBA) at SNS College of Technology is designed to create the next generation of visionary business leaders. Design Thinking in MBA

Business management at SNS is reimagined through Design Thinking. By prototyping innovative business models and strategies, our MBA graduates lead with creativity and empathy.
