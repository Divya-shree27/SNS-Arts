Program Overview

The B.Tech. in Data Sciences at Dr. SNS Rajalakshmi College of Arts and Science is designed to transform students into expert data analysts and statisticians. By integrating **Design Thinking** into the science of data, we enable our students to derive meaningful insights from complex information, driving innovation and strategic decisions in global industries.

Design Thinking in B.Tech. DS

Data science without empathy is just numbers. At SNS, our Design Thinking framework ensures that students first understand the 'why' behind the data. We teach them to empathize with the business and human challenges that data can solve, resulting in insights that are not just accurate, but also actionable and impactful.
