Program Overview

The MBA in Business Analytics at SNS College of Technology bridges the gap between data science and strategic management. Empowered by **Design Thinking**, this program trains students to transform raw data into actionable business insights, enabling organizations to make smarter, empathy-driven decisions in a data-led economy.

Design Thinking in MBA (BA)

Analytics is powerful only when it serves a human purpose. At SNS, our Design Thinking framework ensures that students look beyond the numbers to understand the human behavior and business needs they represent. We teach them to prototype analytical models that solve real-world industrial frustrations, resulting in insights that are both accurate and meaningful.
